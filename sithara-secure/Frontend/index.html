Our Story

Crafted with Passion,

Telugu Abhiruchi was born from a simple belief — that great food is an act of love. Since 2010, our chefs have honoured traditional Telugu recipes while embracing the finest local produce. Every dish on our menu is a bridge between the comfort of home cooking and the richness of Telangana's culinary heritage.
